What is EDA?

  • Exploratory Data Analysis: the process of discovering patterns and relationships within a dataset.
  • the main goals of EDA
    • Uncover the data structure and determine how it is coded.
    • Inspect and "get to know" the data by summarizing and visualizing it.
    • Detect outliers, missing data, and other anomalies and decide how/whether to address these issues.
    • Find new avenues for analysis and further research
    • Prepare for model building or analysis, including the following:
      • Check assumptions
      • Select features
      • Choose an appropriate method
  • EDA techniques
    • Data inspection
        ex. data.head()
    • Numerical summarization
        ex. data.describe(include='all')
    • Data visualization
      • ex. histogram: to inspect the distribution of quantitative feature, providing information about central tendency, spread, and shape (eg., skew or multimodality)
